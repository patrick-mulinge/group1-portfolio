Who We Are

Group 1 is composed of Education Arts students studying History and Kiswahili. The group works collaboratively on academic and practical projects that integrate Information Technology into education.

This portfolio website was created to showcase the group’s existing skills in web design, documentation, and educational content presentation using HTML and CSS.
